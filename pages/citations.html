Citation Policy

To ensure proper attribution and recognition of dataset used in the FOMO60k dataset, we kindly ask all participants to follow the citation policy outlined below.

Main dataset paper: FOMO60K [1] (arxiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.1443).

For the following datasets used in the challenge, participants are required to cite the following:

  • OASIS1, OASIS2 [2,3]
  • Brats24 [4-8]
  • MSD Braintumor [9]
  • IXI [10]
  • MGH Wild [11]
  • NKI [12]

For the following datasets, participants are encouraged to cite the original authors, but it is not mandatory:

  • SOOP [13]
  • NIMH [14]
  • DLBS [15]
  • IDEAS [16]
  • ARC [17]
  • MBSR [18]
  • UCLA [19]
  • QTAB [20]
  • AOMIC ID1000 [21]
